### Outputs Layer

#### Purpose

This folder contains output artifacts generated by the PT Frozen Foods data platform.

It stores results from data analysis, reporting, and intermediate exports.

---

#### Structure

figures/

- charts and visual outputs  

profiling/

- data profiling reports  

reports/

- business and analytical reports  

samples/

- small data samples for demonstration  

tables/

- exported datasets and snapshots  

---

#### Design Principles

- outputs are lightweight and shareable  
- no large datasets should be stored  
- used for analysis, validation, and presentation  

---

#### Notes

- this folder is not part of production pipelines  
- used mainly for development, analysis, and portfolio purposes
